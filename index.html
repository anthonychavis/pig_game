Pig Game Rules

Objective: Be the first player to score 100‚ùó!

Pig Game is a player versus player (PVP) game.

To start the game, player 1 pushes the "Roll Die" button. The player whose turn it is can roll the die as many times as desired, as long as it is still that player's turn. The value of the die will add to the player's Current Score (located in the player's box under the word "Current"), with one exception. If the value of the die is 1, any value in the Current Score is lost and it becomes the next player's turn. To not risk losing the value of the Current Score, the player can select the "Hold" button. This moves the value of the Current Score to the permanent score (located under "Player 1" & "Player 2"). It is then the next player's turn to roll the die. If you're feeling lucky, keep rolling the die to increase your Current Score; just remember to Hold before that unlucky number 1 pops up‚ùó!

Once the value of a player's permanent score is at least 100, that player is the winner of the game üéâ!

Note: At the change of a player's turn due to rolling a 1, the "Roll Die" button will be inactive for a brief period to help prevent that player from rolling for their opponent. After the "Hold" button is used, the "Roll Die" & "Hold" buttons will be inactive for a brief period.
